How can you contribute to our mission

Thank you for considering how you can make a difference in the lives of animals in need. At SFS, we rely on the support of our community to continue our vital work. Here are various ways you can contribute

Your financial contributions help cover the costs of food, shelter, and medical care. Every penny makes a difference.

Give a loving home to one of our animals seeking a forever family. You'll not only change their life but enrich yours in the process.


Join our compassionate team of volunteers. Your time and dedication can have a profound impact on the well-being of our animals.

Participate in or support our fundraising events and campaigns. Together, we can secure a brighter future for our animals.
